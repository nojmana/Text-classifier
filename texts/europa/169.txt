At the Central landfill in Estonia there was a fire, to extinguish attracted members of the defence forces and voluntary rescue workers.As reported by Interfax on Saturday, may 26, in the rescue Department, the alarm center on Friday at 13.54 from the village in Pala parish Kuusalu received a signal about the fire at the Central landfill and explosions.The fire spread rapidly, burned young forest and soil, the smoke has spread to neighboring areas. The fire covered 15 hectares.For reconnaissance were used drone rescue Department. To extinguish, except for rescue workers, attracted by the aircraft of the defence force, military rescue equipment, volunteers.“For residents there is no threat, fighting together with the defence Forces on Saturday will be continued”, – said the head of rescue operations Andrews aasmaa.
